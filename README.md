# CaseConnect – AI-Powered Legal Consultation and Case Management Platform

## Overview

CaseConnect is a legal technology platform designed to connect clients with verified lawyers through a secure and user-friendly digital environment. The platform streamlines the legal consultation process while integrating AI-assisted legal research capabilities to support legal professionals in case analysis and decision-making.

This repository contains the Software Requirements Specification (SRS), system analysis models, UML diagrams, and UI/UX mockups developed during the software engineering and system design phases of the project.

---

## Project Objectives

* Connect clients with qualified and verified lawyers.
* Simplify consultation request and case management processes.
* Enable secure real-time communication between clients and lawyers.
* Provide AI-assisted legal research and case recommendation features.
* Offer administrative tools for platform management and lawyer verification.
